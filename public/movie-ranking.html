Caitlin's Movie Recommendations

My TOP movies of the entire 21 years i've lived.

How To Train Your Dragon

I like the different dragon designs; their overall look and abilities. The worldbuilding is fascinating, the plot really hit the feels as well and it's a movie that always remind me of my childhood.
What can I say? I'm a sucker for nostalgia.

KungFu Panda

Again I really like the different animal designs, how they incorperated the original culture and the musical influence. The art direction was phenomenal in my humble opinion
The plot was also very inspiring with important themes linking to our daily life. These are movies done right.

Night At The Museum

Come on now...The movie speaks for itself.
There won't be a second of boredom watching this. Very interesting with fascinating takes on historical figures. While comedic it has a serious undertone with unexpected moments of frailty and strenght.
Maybe I'm biased, but it's a 10/10.
